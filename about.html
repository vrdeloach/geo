About Us

Info

Hi! We are a small group of Geocachers in Jackson, TN who want to share this amazing activity. We were created after we realized how many Geocaches are located in Jackson and the opportunities this gave us. We meet up a few times a month to go Geocaching together or to just hang out with other people who share this hobby and a love for the outdoors. Everyone is welcome to join and share in the joy of this activity. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us or check out the official Geocaching website.
