Welcome to the Jackson, TN Geocaching Club!

Hi! We are a club of avid Geocachers from Jackson, TN. We want to spread this fun, family friendly, inexpensive way to enjoy nature with families throughout west Tennessee. We have several meetings each month in and around Jackson. We hope to see you at one soon!

Who Are We?

The Jackson Geocaching club aims to be a safe, family friendly place. It is open to everyone, from experienced Geocachers to muggles. You can learn about what Geocaching is, get to know us, and join the club! Please take a look around our website and contact us if you have any questions.

Our main goal in the Jackson Geocaching club is to be an open, welcoming environment for everyone. We will not tolerate any prejudice or harassment. We are here to represent a gentle and loving community that bonds through the wonders of nature.
