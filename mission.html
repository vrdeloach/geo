Our Mission

What We Want to Be

We want this group to be a safe place for our diverse group of members to enjoy Geocaching. Because of this, we ask that you respect our rules and honor our mission. We aim to bring people together and spread the love of Geocaching and respect for nature.

Rules

  • Please make sure you have a Geocaching account! You can make an account for free and download the app or use a handheld gps.
  • Please follow the Geocachers' Creed and follow basic etiquette
  • Cache in, Trash out. Whenever you're geocaching, make sure to pick up any litter in the area and leave it better than you found it!
  • Be respectful! People of all ages, races, and genders deserve to feel safe and welcome.
  • Please try to attend as many meetings as possible! We want to make sure our members are enganged!
