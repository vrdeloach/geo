Fun facts about geocaching
  • There are about 2 million caches worldwide and over 5 million geocachers
  • There are more than 100 geocaches around the world that are only accesible with scuba gear
  • There are more than 126,000 geocaches that can only be found at night
  • A travel bug was sent on the ISS Geocache and made a 350,000,000 mile journey
  • Geocaches in Antarctica have been found more than 175 times

Still confused? Don't worry. Geocaching is an amazing game with a complex, diverse community surrounding it, but it doesn't have to be overwhelming! Check out our FAQ, the official Geocaching blog, or e-mail us! It's okay if you're still a bit confused, no one starts out as an expert.
